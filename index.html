Shri. Prakashdada Solanke

President

The establishment of Shri Shivaji Polytechnic Institute stands as a testament to the unwavering commitment of Marathwada Shikshan Prasarak Mandal towards providing top-notch technical education. This institute embodies the aspirations of countless parents who fervently wish to see their children receive the finest education possible. In an era where quality education has become the cornerstone of success, Shri Shivaji Polytechnic Institute emerges as a beacon of academic excellence. With its dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and innovative teaching methodologies, the institute is poised to shape the future of its students in remarkable ways. Marathwada Shikshan Prasarak Mandal's visionary endeavor to establish this institute echoes the dreams and aspirations of parents who envision a brighter future for their children. Recognizing that education is the key that unlocks countless opportunities, the institute strives to provide an environment that fosters holistic growth, intellectual curiosity, and practical skills. As parents invest their hopes and dreams in the education of their children, Shri Shivaji Polytechnic Institute recognizes this sacred trust and takes it upon itself to deliver on those expectations. The institute not only imparts technical knowledge but also instills values that will guide students throughout their lives. Just as Shivaji Maharaj, a symbol of valor and wisdom.
